       PROCEDURE DIVISION.
           MOVE 5 TO X OF START.
           DISPLAY X OF START.

           MOVE 12 TO Y OF START.
           DISPLAY Y OF START.

           MOVE 17 TO X OF END.
           MOVE 17 TO Y OF END.
           DISPLAY X OF END.
           DISPLAY Y OF END.

      * SHOULD RESULT IN 12,5 FOR END
           SUBTRACT START FROM END.
           DISPLAY X OF END.
           DISPLAY Y OF END.

      * SHOULD RESULT IN 7,-7
           SUBTRACT START FROM END GIVING RESULT.
           DISPLAY X OF RESULT.
           DISPLAY Y OF RESULT.

      * START IS UNCHANGED
           DISPLAY X OF START.
           DISPLAY Y OF START.
